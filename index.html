Women in Computer Science @ Dartmouth College

About Us

We are a community of women interested in computer science and tech at Dartmouth. We hold weekly meetings with a variety of events ranging from professor dinners and pre-professional workshops to community bonding socials. We provide our members with resources and opportunities to help them navigate the computer science curriculum, tech careers, and networking/mentorship.
